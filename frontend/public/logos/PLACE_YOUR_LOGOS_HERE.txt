SnapMagic uses a simple numbered system for logos.
The filename determines the display order from left to right!

‚úÖ SUPPORTED FILENAMES:

- 3.png (displays third)
- 4.png (displays fourth)
- 5.png (displays fifth)
- 6.png (displays sixth, rightmost)

‚úÖ SUPPORTED FORMATS:
- PNG (recommended for transparency)
- JPG/JPEG
- SVG

üéØ HOW IT WORKS:
- Logos are displayed in numerical order (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6)
- All logos are automatically centered as a group
- If you only have 2 logos (1.png, 2.png), they center together
- If you have all 6 logos, they spread across the header
- Missing numbers are skipped (e.g., 1.png, 3.png, 5.png works fine)
